I also learned about [[What is LDAP|LDAP]], [[What is RDP file|RDP file]] and [[What is central directory|central directory]]

> **LDAP** stands for **Lightweight Directory Access Protocol**
> It is a protocol that allows systems and applications to locate, access, and verify information in a centralized directory, such as user credentials, permissions, and device locations across a network

> **RDP file** stands for **Remote Desktop Protocol file**.
> An **RDP file** is a small configuration file that tells your computer **how to connect to another computer using Remote Desktop**.

> A **central directory** is simply **one place where a company stores information about all of its users and devices**.

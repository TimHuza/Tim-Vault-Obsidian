I completed a Mobile Device Management section

**The Four Main Models**
- **BYOD (Bring Your Own Device):**
    - **The Rule:** You use your **own personal phone or laptop** for work.
- **COBO (Corporate-Owned, Business Only):**
    - **The Rule:** The company gives you a device that you can **only use for work**.
- **COPE (Corporate-Owned, Personally Enabled):**
    - **The Rule:** The company owns the device, but they let you use it for **limited personal stuff**.
- **CYOD (Choose Your Own Device):**
	- **The Rule:** The company owns the devices, but they let you **choose your favorite** from an approved list (like picking an iPhone vs. an Android).

| Model    | Who Owns It? | Personal Use Allowed? | Security Level |
| -------- | ------------ | --------------------- | -------------- |
| **BYOD** | Employee     | Yes                   | **Lowest**     |
| **COBO** | Company      | No                    | **Highest**    |
| **COPE** | Company      | Limited               | High           |
| **CYOD** | Company      | Varies                | High           |
